Get to know more about me

In my sophmore year of high school, I took a web design course. With no knowledge whatsoever and little expectations, I entered a completely different world I had never thought I would come to love. In that class I coded for the first time, being introduced to HTML, CSS, and javascript. I even designed a website, for a competition between my classmates, that was chosen. Ever since that class, I knew I could do this forever, but then college happend and I heard so many mixed signals and strayed away from learning web design in an educational setting, but I never let my passion die.

Here I am, today, finally getting my life on track and doing what I always wanted to do without being persuaded by anyone. This is what I love to do, and I am determined to enjoy my life while doing what I love!
